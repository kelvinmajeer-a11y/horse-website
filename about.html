Buyer's Guide

ALL INTERESTED BUYERS PLEASE READ

Good Quality Safe Trail Horses offers a wide range of quality, versatile horses. Our goal is to match each horse with the right owner and make the process smooth and stress free.

Buyer's Responsibility

The buyer is responsible for evaluating the horse before purchase. Please ask any questions about temperament, riding ability, or suitability beforehand.

Coming to Try a Horse

Appointments require a 10% deposit to hold the horse. The deposit is fully refundable if you come see the horse and decide he is not the right fit.

Buying Sight Unseen

Many buyers purchase without visiting. With videos and communication, we help ensure confidence in your decision. If the horse is not as described, options can be discussed.

Vet Checks

We encourage vet checks. Buyers are welcome to arrange exams with a veterinarian of their choice.
