Buyer's Guide

ALL INTERESTED BUYERS PLEASE READ

Good Quality Safe Trail Horses offers a wide range of quality, versatile horses from different disciplines and backgrounds. Our goal is to match each horse with the most suitable owner. We spend a lot of time working with our horses and speaking with prospective buyers to make the buying process smooth and stress free.

Our horses are in high demand, so to keep things fair for all interested buyers, we ask that everyone review the information below before purchasing.

Buyer's Responsibility

The buyer has the responsibility to carefully evaluate a horse before committing to purchase. If you have any concerns about conformation, temperament, stall manners, size, riding ability, or any other detail, please ask questions before making a purchase.

Buying a horse without asking questions or doing proper research means the responsibility lies with the buyer. We are always happy to answer questions and provide as much information as possible to help you make the best decision.

Coming to Try a Horse

Our horses are exposed to many different environments and situations while in our program so we can determine the type of rider or program they are best suited for.

Every horse is different, which is why we provide detailed descriptions and videos showing their abilities. When you come to try a horse in person, it allows both of us to determine whether the horse is the right match for you.

Appointments to try horses are available Monday through Friday and you are welcome to bring your trainer with you.

Buying Sight Unseen

Many of our horses are purchased by buyers who are unable to visit in person. Because of this, we provide detailed videos and honest descriptions to help buyers make confident decisions.

We regularly help arrange transportation across the United States. We are happy to speak with you about your riding experience, goals, and what you are looking for in a horse so we can help match you with the right partner.

Vet Checks

We always encourage vet checks if it makes you more comfortable with your purchase. Buyers are welcome to schedule an exam with a veterinarian of their choice.

While we take pride in representing our horses honestly, we still recommend buyers complete any exams or evaluations they feel necessary before purchase.
